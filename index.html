2D LBM D2Q9 + Smagorinsky LES – Channel Flow over a Joukowsky Airfoil

CPU-based LBM D2Q9 solver with Smagorinsky LES in lattice units. Joukowsky airfoil as immersed obstacle (bounce-back); velocity magnitude rendering.

Simulation Parameters

Flow Speed (Mach) 0.10
Relaxation Time τ (base) 0.80
Smagorinsky Constant Cs 0.16
Angle of Attack (deg) 5°
Airfoil Thickness (t/c) 0.12
Camber 0.08

Aerodynamic Estimate (Thin-Airfoil)

α 5.0° (0.087 rad)
CL ≈ 2π α 0.55

LBM is solved in lattice units (Δx = Δt = 1, cs² = 1/3). CL shown here is the analytical thin-airfoil estimate, while the LBM field provides qualitative pressure/velocity structures.

Grid: 300×120 nodes, channel flow with no-slip walls and Joukowsky airfoil as bounce-back obstacle. For stability keep Mach < 0.2 and τ > 0.55.
